Vegetarian Lasagna

A hearty and flavorful lasagna packed with layers of vegetables and cheese.

Ingredients

  • 9-12 lasagna noodles (depending on the size of your dish)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
  • 1 cup spinach, chopped
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish (optional)

Instructions

1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).

2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.

3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic, and sauté until softened.

4. Add the zucchini, bell pepper, and mushrooms, cooking until the vegetables are tender. Stir in the spinach and cook until wilted.

5. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, oregano, salt, and pepper.

6. In a baking dish, spread a layer of marinara sauce on the bottom. Place a layer of lasagna noodles, followed by half of the ricotta mixture, half of the vegetable mixture, and a layer of mozzarella cheese.

7. Repeat the layers (sauce, noodles, ricotta, vegetables, mozzarella) until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of noodles topped with marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese.

8. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese on top.

9. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.

10. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh basil if desired, and enjoy your delicious vegetarian lasagna!
