Banana Smoothie

A creamy, delicious, and healthy smoothie to kickstart your day.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup of milk (or almond milk)
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • A handful of ice cubes

Instructions

1. Peel the bananas and break them into chunks.

2. In a blender, combine the bananas, milk, yogurt, honey, vanilla extract, and ice cubes.

3. Blend everything until smooth and creamy.

4. Pour into glasses and serve immediately. Enjoy your refreshing banana smoothie!
