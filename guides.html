Ingredient Guides

Fruits

Fruits are essential for a balanced diet. They are packed with vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants. Here are some popular fruits:

  • Apples
  • Bananas
  • Oranges
  • Berries (strawberries, blueberries, etc.)
  • Grapes

Vegetables

Vegetables are a key part of any healthy meal. They provide essential nutrients and fiber. Common vegetables include:

  • Carrots
  • Spinach
  • Broccoli
  • Bell Peppers
  • Zucchini

Grains

Grains are a great source of energy and nutrients. They are vital for a balanced diet. Popular grains include:

  • Rice
  • Quinoa
  • Oats
  • Pasta
  • Bread

Proteins

Proteins are crucial for muscle repair and growth. Include these protein sources in your meals:

  • Chicken
  • Fish
  • Eggs
  • Beans
  • Nuts and seeds

Dairy

Dairy products are rich in calcium and vitamins. Consider adding these to your diet:

  • Milk
  • Yogurt
  • Cheese
