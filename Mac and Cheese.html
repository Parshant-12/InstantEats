Mac and Cheese

A classic comfort food dish made with creamy cheese sauce and tender macaroni pasta.

Ingredients

  • 200g elbow macaroni
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika (optional)
  • Breadcrumbs for topping (optional)

Instructions

1. Cook the elbow macaroni according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

2. In a large saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the flour, whisking continuously for about 1 minute to create a roux.

3. Gradually pour in the milk, whisking until smooth. Continue to cook until the mixture thickens, about 5 minutes.

4. Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in the shredded cheddar cheese until melted and smooth.

5. Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce, mixing until well coated. Season with salt, pepper, and paprika if using.

6. If desired, transfer to a baking dish, sprinkle breadcrumbs on top, and broil for a few minutes until golden brown.

7. Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Mac and Cheese!
