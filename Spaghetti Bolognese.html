Spaghetti Bolognese

A classic Italian dish with a rich, meaty sauce served over spaghetti.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 300g ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  • 1 carrot, grated
  • 1 celery stalk, chopped
  • 400g canned tomatoes (or passata)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving (optional)

Instructions

1. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

2. In a large pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and garlic, and saut√© until softened.

3. Add the ground beef to the pan and cook until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon.

4. Stir in the grated carrot and chopped celery, and cook for a few more minutes.

5. Add the canned tomatoes, tomato paste, salt, pepper, and oregano. Bring to a simmer and cook for about 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally.

6. Once the sauce has thickened, combine it with the cooked spaghetti.

7. Serve hot, garnished with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese if desired. Enjoy your delicious Spaghetti Bolognese!
