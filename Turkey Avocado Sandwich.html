Turkey Avocado Sandwich

A delicious and nutritious sandwich packed with protein and healthy fats!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole grain or sourdough bread
  • 4-6 slices deli turkey
  • 1/2 avocado, sliced
  • 1 slice cheddar or Swiss cheese (optional)
  • 1/2 cup lettuce or spinach
  • 2 slices tomato
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Your favorite mustard or mayo

Instructions

1. Toast the bread slices until golden brown, if desired.

2. Spread mustard or mayo on one or both slices of bread.

3. Layer the turkey slices on one slice of bread.

4. Top with sliced avocado, cheese (if using), lettuce, and tomato.

5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

6. Place the other slice of bread on top to complete the sandwich.

7. Cut in half and serve with your favorite side, such as chips or a salad.
